## Scope

These terms apply to the CTXBench Lattes dataset package, including curriculum-derived artifacts such as:

- `raw.html`;
- `clean.html`;
- `parsed.json`;
- `blocks.json`;
- packaged dataset archives;
- dataset manifests and checksums.

These terms do not replace the repository software license. Source code, scripts, benchmark questions, experiment configurations, documentation, and other original benchmark materials may be covered by separate licenses.

## Purpose

CTXBench Lattes is provided for academic research, benchmarking, and reproducibility purposes.

The dataset is intended to support research on context access and provisioning strategies for LLM-based systems. It should be used to evaluate systems and methods, not the individuals represented in the original curriculum pages.

The dataset contains artifacts derived from public Brazilian Lattes curriculum pages.

The dataset maintainers do not claim ownership over the original curriculum content. The original content remains associated with its respective authors, sources, and platforms.

The maintainers claim authorship only over the benchmark-specific materials they created, such as:

- experiment configurations;
- metadata;
- scripts;
- dataset organization;
- derived benchmark packaging.
