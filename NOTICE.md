# NOTICE

CTXBench Lattes is a benchmark dataset instance for evaluating context access and provisioning strategies in LLM-based systems.

## Source of Curriculum-Derived Artifacts

The dataset includes artifacts derived from public Brazilian Lattes curriculum pages.

The Lattes Platform is maintained by CNPq, the Brazilian National Council for Scientific and Technological Development.

The dataset maintainers are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by CNPq or the Lattes Platform.

## Third-Party Content

Curriculum-derived artifacts may contain content authored by the respective curriculum owners or obtained from public curriculum records.

The CTXBench maintainers do not claim ownership over the original curriculum content.

The benchmark-specific organization, metadata, questions, configuration files, scripts, and packaging were created for research and reproducibility purposes.

## Responsible Use

The dataset is provided for academic research, benchmarking, and reproducibility.

It must not be used to make decisions about individuals, rank researchers, evaluate careers, infer sensitive personal attributes, or support profiling.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use complies with applicable laws, institutional policies, research ethics requirements, and platform terms.

## Dataset Transformations

The dataset may include transformed representations of public curriculum pages, such as:

- raw HTML;
- cleaned HTML;
- parsed JSON;
- semantic context blocks.

These transformations are intended to support controlled evaluation of LLM context provisioning strategies.

Transformations may introduce errors, omissions, or structural differences relative to the original curriculum pages.
